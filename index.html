Biography

Matin Najafi is a first year Ph.D. student at the University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Professor Reynold Cheng. His research is centered around the analysis of large interconnected systems focusing on modeling complex graphs, and networks for systems at all scales, from interactions of proteins in a cell to interactions between humans in a society. He is currently designing and developing a new Network Motif Discovery Algorithm.
